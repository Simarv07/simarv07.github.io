My Story

I was raised in Surrey, BC Canada, and have called it my home since kindergarten. Throughout my academic years, I attended Enver Creek Secondary, where I found a keen interest in engineering and coding. I was fascinated by robotics and took various computer programming courses, which ignited my passion for technology even further.

After graduating high school, I decided to pursue my passion for technology and enrolled in the Computer Systems Technology Diploma program at the British Columbia Institute of Technology (BCIT). I was determined to learn everything I could about computer systems and technology, and my dedication paid off when I was accepted into the Co-op program at BCIT.

During my Co-op term, I worked as a data engineer at the Insurance Corporation of British Columbia (ICBC) for eight months. It was a challenging yet rewarding experience that taught me valuable skills and allowed me to apply my knowledge in a real-world setting. I am grateful for the opportunity to have worked at such a prestigious organization and for the knowledge and skills I acquired during my time there. I am now looking forward to completing my diploma in December 2023 and pursuing a career in the technology industry with the support of my family and friends.
