My Story

I was raised in Surrey, BC, and have called it home since kindergarten. My passion for engineering and coding began at Enver Creek Secondary, where I explored robotics and computer programming.

After high school, I pursued the Computer Systems Technology program at BCIT, gaining hands-on experience through a Co-op term as a Data Engineer at ICBC, where I worked on large-scale data processing systems. I then served as Lead Backend Developer at TerraTap Technologies, where I led the development of scalable APIs and data infrastructure for global datasets.

In early 2024, I worked as a Software Engineer at Purple Lotus, focusing on internal tools and cloud-based infrastructure. Currently, I’m a Junior Software Engineer at Westgate Technology Corp., where I’m developing a backend system and user dashboard to manage and monitor multiple autonomous systems in real-time. Alongside work, I am completing a part-time Bachelor's degree in Applied Computer Science at BCIT, specializing in databases and software architecture.
